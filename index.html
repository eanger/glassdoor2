    Were the xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx Design Thinking Team. GBS is xxxx services consulting arm, and were charged with infusing xxx Design Thinking throughout the organization. Were currently engaged in a number of projects to transform GBS, and we need some help. Our immediate need is on the team designing an experience to engage with xxx clients and educate them on the possibilities of automation. The ideal candidate will be able to lead the UX of the digital components, participate in the development of the in-person elements, and eventually take a leadership role in delivering the experience to the clients.

    This is a great opportunity to opportunity to work closely with xxx SMEs from around the world, clients from companies outside of xxx, and your design team peers to craft thoughtful, intuitive solutions.

    Responsibilities include:
    • Ensure there is consistency and orchestration in the design strategy as they apply design to experiences, products and services.
    • Produce mood boards, storyboards, design briefs, user flows, prototypes, and other artifacts required to develop and evolve user experience designs.
    • Work with design team to lobby for and plan evaluation of prototypes to ensure a delightful user experience.
    • Defend design choices using previous user research, heuristics and usability standards.
    • Create world-class user experiences that support and reinforce xxx vision, brand and business objectives across all platforms.
    • Provide support during discussions with clients and help assess design needs for projects
    • Understand the larger strategic goal and understanding how to work within that constraint
    • Develop mid-fi and hi-fi mockups when necessary
    • Rapidly build and iterate prototypes
    • Grow into a workshop leadership role
    • This position will include some amount of international travel
    Skills Required:
    • Is empathetic and attuned to users' needs, motivations, and behaviors
    • An appreciation that experience extends beyond the screen
    • Natural curiosity and comfort with ambiguity accompanied by an extreme bias towards making and a willingness to learn
    • Must be able to frame user opportunities within the context of business needs and effectively communicate with & enroll extended team to address those opportunities
    • Is capable of developing a conceptual model and delivering concrete experiences in service of that model
    • Must be able to deliver delightful, thoughtful design solutions that are relevant to our users and our business objectives
    • Must have the ability to successfully negotiate and evolve the design with stakeholders and the working team, to ensure the integrity of the design is upheld in the delivered product or service
    • Strong modern UX skillset.
    • Demonstrated knowledge of best practices in UX Patterns, web standards, mobile design patterns.
    • Demonstrated ability to create click-through prototypes to test and evaluate working hypothesis with users.
    • Working knowledge of how compositions translate to HTML, JavaScript and CSS used to deliver feasible designs.
    • Knowledge of accessible designs.
    • Ability to work with brand and design guidelines.
    • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills, including techniques to persuade and negotiate and the ability to articulate alternative approaches.
    • Outstanding storytelling and presentation skills.
    • Exceptional attention to detail.
    Required Technical and Professional Expertise
    • 2+ year of UX design experience
    • Knowledge of design tools including Adobe Creative Suite
    • Visual portfolio that displays a solid understanding of design principles and interface design
    • Readiness to travel 15% annually
    • English: Fluent
